Run Your Own Mini Shop
Set up a pretend shop, price your items, and practise giving change using Australian money.
👆 Tap to see your steps ▾
Your Steps 🪜
  • 1Choose 6–8 items to "sell" and give each a price under $2.
  • 2Make a price list or menu for your shop.

Music
Compose a Rhythm Pattern
Create your own rhythm pattern using claps, taps, and body percussion — then teach it to someone!
👆 Tap to see your steps ▾
Your Steps 🪜
  • 1Choose 3 sounds: clap, stomp, pat knees, or click fingers.
  • 2Create a pattern of 8 beats using your 3 sounds.
